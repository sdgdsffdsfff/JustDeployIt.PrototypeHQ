Caching your repository

Whenever you tell us about one of your repositories we have to store a copy of it on our servers. This allows us to quickly access your commit history and files whenever you wish to deploy the project. If you have a large repository and you may need to wait for it to finish before you can run your first deployment.

If you change your repository URL at any point, we'll also need to download a new repository. This process can take time too so please be patient while waiting for it to happen.

We store all your information securely on our servers and full information can be found in our privacy policy.
